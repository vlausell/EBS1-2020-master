English for Business Studies I

English for Business Studies I (EBS1)is a course aimed at university students with an upper-intermediate to advanced level of English who are interested in eventually working in international business. The course topics have been selected considering their relevance in today’s international business environment. The aims of the course are to:
• enable the student to carry out a wide range of communicative tasks within an international professional context in which he or she may one day be working,
• improve the students’ reading and listening comprehension through the use of authentic recordings and texts,
• introduce the vocabulary and register necessary to function within a professional context,
• review and build on essential English grammar, going over most problematic areas of grammar and syntax of the language used in business,
• help students develop strategies so that they are in control of their own learning.
